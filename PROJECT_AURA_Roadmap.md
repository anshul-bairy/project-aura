## ANTI-DRIFT SYSTEM (built for: "I drift when it's hard")

- **The 25-minute floor.** The smallest unit is one 25-min focused block. Bad day = just one block. That still counts. Don't moralise the small days.
- **The Wall Rule.** The moment you hit something confusing and feel the urge to bail → you do **NOT** close the laptop. You switch to *notes mode* and write the confusing thing down as a question in your own words. Frustration becomes a note. This keeps you in the chair, which is the whole game.
- **The Chain.** Your daily post = proof you learned something = your streak. Miss a day and there's a visible gap. Same dopamine as an Instagram streak, pointed at your future.
- **Record-to-focus.** You can't drift while the webcam is rolling. The camera isn't just content — it's a focus cage. That's its real job.
